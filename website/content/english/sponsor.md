## Help Build the Future of Holons

Have a feature you'd love to see in Holons? You can **sponsor its development** and help prioritize what gets built next.

### How It Works

1. **Request a feature** — Join the [Holonic DAO Telegram community](https://t.me/HolonicDAO) and share your idea
2. **Community discussion** — The community discusses and refines the proposal
3. **Fund it** — Contribute to the feature through our [OpenCollective page](https://opencollective.com/holoniclabs)
4. **Track progress** — Watch as sponsored features move up the priority list and get built

### Why Sponsor?

- **Direct impact** — Your contribution directly funds the development of the feature you care about
- **Community-driven** — Features are shaped by real user needs, not top-down decisions
- **Transparent** — All funds are managed transparently through OpenCollective
