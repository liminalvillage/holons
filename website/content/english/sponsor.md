## Help Build the Future of Holons

Have a feature you'd love to see in Holons? You can **sponsor its development** and help prioritize what gets built next.

### How It Works

1. **Request a feature** — Join the [Holonic DAO Telegram community](https://t.me/HolonicDAO) and share your idea
2. **Community discussion** — The community discusses and refines the proposal
3. **Fund it** — Contribute to the feature through our [OpenCollective page](https://opencollective.com/holoniclabs)
4. **Track progress** — Watch as sponsored features move up the priority list and get built

### Why Sponsor?

- **Direct impact** — Your contribution directly funds the development of the feature you care about
- **Community-driven** — Features are shaped by real user needs, not top-down decisions
- **Transparent** — All funds are managed transparently through OpenCollective

## Contribute as a Developer

Holons is **open source** and has been incubated at [Liminal Village](https://www.liminalvillage.com), a regenerative community in the Marche region of Italy. The technology powers the [Regenerativa network](https://regenerativa.earth) — a growing ecosystem of bioregional communities practicing sustainable living, circular economy, and social innovation.

### What's in it for you?

Committed open source developers who contribute to Holons get:

- 🏡 **Free stay at Liminal Village** — Live and work from a beautiful location in rural Italy while contributing to meaningful technology
- 🌍 **Access to networked locations** — Connect with other communities in the Regenerativa network across multiple countries
- 🤝 **A real community** — You're not just writing code in isolation; you're building tools used daily by people living regeneratively
- 🌱 **Meaningful work** — Help build decentralized coordination tools that empower communities worldwide

### How to Get Involved

1. Check out the [Holons repo on GitHub](https://github.com/liminalvillage/holons)
2. Join the [Holonic DAO Telegram channel](https://t.me/HolonicDAO) to connect with the team
3. Pick up an issue or propose a feature — and start contributing
4. Visit [Liminal Village](https://www.liminalvillage.com) to meet the community in person
