Contribute as a Developer

Holons is open source and has been incubated at Liminal Village, a regenerative community in the Marche region of Italy. The technology powers the Regenerativa network — a growing ecosystem of bioregional communities practicing sustainable living, circular economy, and social innovation.

Committed open source developers who contribute to Holons get:

  • 🏡 Free stay at Liminal Village — Live and work from a beautiful location in rural Italy
  • 🌍 Access to networked locations — Connect with communities across the Regenerativa network
  • 🤝 A real community — Build tools used daily by people living regeneratively
  • 🌱 Meaningful work — Decentralized coordination tools empowering communities worldwide
